Abdulhaqq Omoyele

I'm a writer who shifts base between Lagos and Bauchi. I like to write about politics, culture, and career. The most interesting things about me are that I can spin things on my fingers and that I'm a living, breathing mass of human contradictions. I am extremely aquaphobic even though I'm a good swimmer and I enjoy chopping and dicing vegetables even though I have a paralysing fear of razors and sharp things (yes, there's a story behind it). I've had brief internships and volunteer roles but most of my work experience has been freelance.
